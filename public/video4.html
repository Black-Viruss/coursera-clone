Introduction to quiz on resource allocation

The purpose of this quiz is to suggest a resource allocation. Follow the instructions and respond to the questions. This is a required activity for the module.

You will analyze the resource allocation of Fruit4Every1, a producer of fruits. A reading is provided with estimates on how much each business is worth (“enterprise value”) as part of Fruit4Every1 (“before spin-off”) and would be worth if separate from Fruit4Every1 (“after spin-off”). Recall that a spin-off is when a company divests a business by distributing the shares of that business to the shareholders.
